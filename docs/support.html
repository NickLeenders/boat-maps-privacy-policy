Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Overview

Boat Maps is a waterway navigation app for Amsterdam. We take your privacy seriously and collect as little data as possible.

Location Data

Boat Maps uses your device's GPS to display your position on the map and provide turn-by-turn navigation. Your precise location is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

When you view the map, the visible map area (a bounding box of the region on screen) is sent to our server to load nearby points of interest such as restaurants and docks. This does not identify you or your precise location.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information
  • We do not track your movement history or routes
  • We do not share any data with advertisers or analytics services
  • We do not use third-party tracking or analytics SDKs

Third-Party Services

Boat Maps uses Supabase to serve map features (points of interest). Supabase receives map viewport coordinates to respond to queries. See Supabase's privacy policy for details.

Children's Privacy

Boat Maps does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
